Frequently asked

How far in advance should I book my bridal date?

For peak wedding seasons we recommend reserving 3–6 months ahead. A booking deposit secures your date and your dedicated lead artist. We do accommodate shorter notice when availability allows.

Do you offer bridal trials?

Yes. Our Signature Bride and Grand Trousseau packages include private trial sessions so we can perfect your look — palette, longevity and styling — well before the day.

Are your keratin and hair treatments formaldehyde-free?

Always. We use premium, formaldehyde-free smoothing systems chosen for safety and a healthy, glass-glossy finish that lasts.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

We offer on-location and destination bridal styling across Pakistan. Travel is quoted bespoke — share your dates and venue and we'll compose a proposal.

What products and brands do you use?

We work exclusively with professional, internationally recognised makeup, hair and skincare lines, selected per client for the finest, most flattering results.

How do I pay and is a deposit required?

Bridal and package bookings require a deposit to confirm the date, with the balance settled on the day. We accept cash, card and bank transfer.
